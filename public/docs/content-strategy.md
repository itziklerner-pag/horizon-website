#### Technology > How Fuel Cells Work

**Primary Goal:** Educate buyers on technology without overwhelming; build confidence

**Hero Section**
- **Headline:** "Clean, Efficient Power Through Electrochemistry"
- **Subheadline:** "Fuel cells convert hydrogen to electricity with zero emissions and superior efficiency"

**Simple Explanation (Audience: Non-Technical)**
- **Analogy:** "Like a battery that never runs out, as long as you supply hydrogen"
- **Basic Process:**
  1. Hydrogen enters the fuel cell
  2. Electrochemical reaction splits hydrogen into protons and electrons
  3. Electrons flow through circuit (electricity)
  4. Protons combine with oxygen to form water
  5. Heat is byproduct (captured for efficiency)
- **Simple Diagram:** Visual showing hydrogen + oxygen → electricity + water + heat

**Technical Deep Dive (Audience: Engineers)**
- **Toggle to "Technical View"**
- PEM fuel cell chemistry details
- Stack architecture and materials
- Thermodynamic efficiency explanation
- Balance-of-plant components (air compressor, cooling, controls)

**Advantages Over Alternatives**

**vs. Combustion (Diesel/Natural Gas)**
- No combustion = no NOx, PM, CO
- Higher efficiency (45-50% vs. 30-35%)
- Silent operation
- Lower maintenance (no reciprocating parts)

**vs. Batteries**
- Unlimited runtime with fuel supply
- No capacity degradation over time
- Faster refueling than recharging
- Higher energy density for large applications

**Interactive Comparison Tool**
- Slider showing power output, runtime, emissions for fuel cell vs. diesel vs. battery

## 8. CONTENT PRODUCTION & GOVERNANCE

**Recommended Roles:**
1. **Content Strategy Lead** (1 FTE)
   - Owns overall content strategy and calendar
   - Coordinates across product marketing, demand gen, sales enablement
   - Measures content performance and ROI

2. **Technical Writers** (2 FTE)
   - Create whitepapers, technical briefs, case studies, product documentation
   - Work closely with engineering and product teams
   - Ensure technical accuracy

3. **SEO/Blog Writer** (1 FTE)
   - Produce 2-3 SEO-optimized blog posts per week
   - Keyword research and on-page optimization
   - Track search rankings and organic traffic

4. **Multimedia Producer** (1 FTE)
   - Video production (scripting, shooting, editing)
   - Infographics and visual content
   - Interactive tools and calculators

5. **Content Designer** (1 FTE)
   - Layout and design of whitepapers, case studies, one-pagers
   - Website content design (landing pages, resource pages)
   - Brand consistency

6. **Editor/Content Manager** (0.5 FTE)
   - Edit all content for quality, clarity, consistency
   - Manage content calendar and deadlines
   - Publish and distribute content

**External Support:**
- Freelance industry writers for guest posts and bylined articles
- SEO agency for link building and technical SEO
- Video production agency for high-production-value customer testimonials

### Content Calendar & Cadence

**Monthly Content Production:**
- 8-12 blog posts (SEO-focused)
- 1-2 whitepapers or case studies (gated, high-value)
- 1-2 technical briefs or comparison guides
- 1 webinar (live)
- 2-4 videos (product demos, customer testimonials, explainers)
- 5-10 social media posts per week (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- 1 email newsletter (to subscriber list)
- Multiple nurture email campaigns (automated)

**Quarterly Content Initiatives:**
- Major thought leadership report or industry study
- Updated cost calculators and interactive tools
- Refreshed case studies with new customer results
- New webinar series launch
- Trade show content and collateral

**Annual Content Initiatives:**
- Comprehensive content audit and refresh
- SEO strategy review and keyword research update
- Brand refresh (if needed)
- Customer satisfaction survey and testimonial collection
- Competitive analysis and battle card updates

### Content Quality Standards

**Editorial Guidelines:**
- Tone: Professional, authoritative, but approachable (not overly technical for general audience)
- Voice: First-person plural ("we at Horizon") for company voice; neutral third-person for educational content
- Grammar and style: Follow AP Style for general content, IEEE standards for technical content
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for web content

**Technical Accuracy:**
- All technical claims reviewed by engineering team
- Performance data verified against actual installations
- Customer results approved by customers before publication
- Regular updates to reflect latest product specs and performance

**Brand Consistency:**
- Use approved terminology (e.g., "fuel cell system" not "fuel cell power plant")
- Consistent naming of products and solutions
- Approved boilerplate language for company descriptions
- Brand guidelines for visual elements (colors, fonts, imagery)

### Content Performance Metrics

**Traffic Metrics:**
- Website traffic (sessions, unique visitors)
- Traffic by source (organic, paid, referral, direct)
- Top-performing pages (by traffic)
- Bounce rate and time on page
- Conversion rate by page

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Content downloads (whitepapers, case studies)
- Webinar registrations and attendance
- Video views and completion rates
- Email open and click-through rates
- Social media engagement (likes, shares, comments)

**Lead Generation Metrics:**
- Leads generated by content asset
- Lead quality (MQL rate, SQL rate)
- Cost per lead by content type
- Lead source attribution
- Conversion rate from lead to opportunity to customer

**SEO Metrics:**
- Keyword rankings (target keywords)
- Organic traffic growth
- Backlinks acquired (number, quality)
- Domain Authority (DA)
- Top organic landing pages

**Business Impact Metrics:**
- Pipeline generated from content marketing
- Revenue influenced by content
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) with content vs. without
- Sales cycle length (impact of content on acceleration)

**Content ROI Calculation:**
- ROI = (Revenue Attributed to Content - Content Production Cost) / Content Production Cost
- Track by content type, campaign, and channel

### Content Distribution Strategy

**Owned Channels:**
- Website (blog, resource library)
- Email (newsletters, nurture campaigns)
- Customer portal (for existing customers)

**Earned Channels:**
- SEO (organic search traffic)
- Industry publications (guest posts, contributed articles)
- Press coverage (media mentions, articles)
- Customer referrals and word-of-mouth

**Paid Channels:**
- LinkedIn Ads (sponsored content, lead gen forms)
- Google Ads (search ads for high-intent keywords)
- Retargeting ads (display ads to website visitors)
- Sponsored content in industry publications

**Partner Channels:**
- Co-marketing with hydrogen suppliers, electrical contractors, data center operators
- Industry association content sharing (7x24, AFCOM)
- Reseller and partner portals

**Distribution Tactics:**
- Repurpose content across formats (whitepaper → blog series → infographic → video → social posts)
- Syndicate content to industry sites (with canonical tags for SEO)
- Share customer case studies on customer's website and social channels
- Email segmentation for personalized content delivery
- Social media scheduling and promotion

### Testing & Optimization

**A/B Testing Priorities:**
1. Homepage hero headline and CTA
2. Landing page layouts and form lengths
3. Email subject lines and CTAs
4. CTA button copy and placement
5. Pricing transparency (showing ranges vs. "contact us")
6. Trust indicators (placement of logos, testimonials)

**Testing Methodology:**
- Minimum 2-week test duration (or until statistical significance)
- Single-variable tests (isolate one change at a time)
- Document hypotheses and learnings
- Implement winners and iterate

**Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Roadmap:**
- Month 1-2: Baseline measurement and heat mapping analysis
- Month 3-4: Form optimization and friction reduction
- Month 5-6: CTA testing across key pages
- Month 7-8: Landing page redesigns for top campaigns
- Ongoing: Continuous iterative testing

   - Analyze trends and patterns

   - Review existing content for accuracy and freshness
   - Identify gaps in buyer journey coverage
   - Check for broken links and outdated information

3. **SEO Health Check**
   - Review keyword rankings and organic traffic
   - Analyze backlink profile for quality
   - Technical SEO audit (site speed, mobile-friendliness, indexing)

4. **Competitive Analysis**
   - Review competitor content and SEO strategies
   - Identify opportunities to differentiate
   - Update competitive battle cards

5. **Strategy Adjustment**
   - Based on learnings, adjust content priorities
   - Reallocate resources to higher-performing content types
   - Update messaging based on customer feedback and market changes

6. **Planning Next Quarter**
   - Set goals and priorities for next quarter
   - Update content calendar
   - Align with product launches and company initiatives
